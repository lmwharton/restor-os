## Overview

**Problem:** Equipment is tracked informally (count × days for billing). No inventory management, no ROI tracking. Drying certificates are generated manually or not at all — but they're required to close out mitigation billing.

**Solution:** Equipment library with deployment tracking + auto-generated drying certificate from existing moisture readings data.

## Phases & Checklist

### Phase 1: Equipment Inventory — ❌
- [ ] `equipment_library` table: company_id, equipment_type (air_mover/dehu/air_scrubber/etc), brand, model, serial_number, status (available/deployed/maintenance)
- [ ] Company Settings → Inventory tab: manage equipment library
- [ ] Track which equipment is deployed on which job/room
- [ ] Equipment ROI tracking: which units generate most billing
- [ ] pytest: equipment CRUD, deployment tracking

### Phase 2: Drying Certificate — ❌
- [ ] `POST /v1/jobs/:id/drying-certificate` — generate drying certificate PDF
- [ ] `GET /v1/jobs/:id/drying-certificate/download` — download generated certificate
- [ ] Auto-generated from moisture_readings + job_rooms data (collected in Spec 01)
- [ ] Certificate format (from Brett's ScopeFlow demo):
  - Header: "Certificate of Dryness" + cert number (CERT-XXXXX) + issue date
  - Property address, claim number, loss type
  - Date of loss, drying period (start → end), days of drying
  - Technician, adjuster, contractor fields
  - Status badge: "DRYING IN PROGRESS" or "DRYING COMPLETE"
  - Room-by-room drying summary table: room name, dimensions, drying period, dry standard, final readings (color-coded: orange if above std, green if at/below), equipment used, status (IN PROGRESS / COMPLETE)
  - Statement of Compliance: "This certifies that the above-referenced property has been inspected and treated for water damage by the restoration contractor in accordance with the IICRC S500 Standard for Professional Water Damage Restoration. Moisture readings were recorded daily and equipment was maintained throughout the drying period."
  - Technician signature line + date of issue
  - Footer: "Generated by Crewmatic | IICRC S500 Compliant Documentation"
- [ ] Determines completion: all rooms' final readings ≤ dry standard → status = COMPLETE
- [ ] Needed to close out mitigation billing — proves equipment days were justified
- [ ] pytest: certificate generates correctly from moisture data
- [ ] pytest: status correctly determines IN PROGRESS vs COMPLETE
